Engineering Support for Rural Counties, Agencies, Farms, and Building Owners

JYA Inc. provides rural engineering services to counties, state agencies, federal programs, and private rural building owners — including farms, co‑ops, and small facilities. We bring federal‑grade engineering discipline to the practical realities of rural communities.

Procurement Reality

How Rural Communities Procure Engineering Services

Rural jurisdictions rarely rely on large procurement portals. Opportunities are typically announced through:

  • County and municipal websites
  • Clerk or administrative notices
  • Public works and facilities postings
  • Local newspaper legal notices
  • Occasional state or federal procurement systems

JYA Rural Engineering operates comfortably in this environment — straightforward scopes, practical expectations, and responsive support.
