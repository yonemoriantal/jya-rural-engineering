About

Rural Engineering Without Urban Complexity

JYA Inc. provides practical engineering support for rural counties, municipalities, farms, co-ops, and private rural building owners.

We bring disciplined technical guidance to public works, agricultural facilities, and rural infrastructure — without large-firm overhead.

We also support HOMES and HEAR energy rebate programs, helping rural homeowners, contractors, and property owners access federal incentives for energy-efficient upgrades.
