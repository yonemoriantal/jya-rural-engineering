About

Program Focus

The USDA Rural Engineering Division provides technical, engineering, and program alignment support for rural infrastructure initiatives administered through USDA Rural Development and related agencies.

Support focuses on practical, review‑ready documentation, cost alignment, and infrastructure feasibility across rural and underserved communities.

This division operates as a program‑specific capability of JYA Inc., leveraging federal, defense, and infrastructure experience to support rural program implementation.
