About

Program Focus

The USDA Rural Engineering Division provides technical and program-aligned support for energy, water, broadband, and community infrastructure initiatives administered through USDA Rural Development.

Work emphasizes feasibility, cost alignment, and review-ready documentation to support successful funding and implementation across rural America.
